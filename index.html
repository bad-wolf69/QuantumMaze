Quantum Maze

An electronic circuit board for a maze, help the electron find its way!

View project on GitHub

Paradigm Shift brings you Quantum Maze! A light 2D maze game that keeps you busy for a while. Ever wondered how an electron finds its way to the end of the line? Well here's a game that lets you follow its path, help guide our hero, the electron, through its journey inside an electronic circuit board! A accelerometer dependent game, that challenges your cognitive skills. Are you ready?

Touch the sides and you shall lose your charge and die, guide it fast and in time to gain stars, challenge your friends today!
